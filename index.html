Bio

I am a Research Scientist at the Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI) in Italy. My research bridges formal methods and software engineering, focusing on automated program repair (APR), fault localization, and software verification. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Western Australia and held research positions at Simula Research Laboratory, NUS, and NTU.

Research

My research focuses on automated program analysis, program repair, model checking, static analysis, and fault localization, aiming to improve reliability and scalability of software tools for real-world applications.

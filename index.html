Research

Dr. Omar I. Al-Bataineh's research spans formal methods and software engineering, focusing on model checking, static analysis, runtime verification, and automated program repair. His work aims to integrate formal verification techniques into practical software development processes to enhance reliability and robustness, particularly in multi-fault program scenarios. He develops scalable, theoretically grounded methods for improving the automation of program repair and bridging the gap between formal methods and mainstream software engineering.

For a comprehensive list of his publications, visit his DBLP profile. You can also download his curriculum vitae for more details about his academic background and experience.
